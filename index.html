Privacy Policy — CallBlockery

Last updated: May 5, 2025

CallBlockery ("we", "our", or "the app") is a call-blocking application for Android. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

CallBlockery collects only the minimum data necessary to provide its service:

  • Phone numbers you add to your blocklist: Stored locally on your device only. Never transmitted to our servers.
  • Blocked call history: A log of calls blocked by the app, stored locally on your device only.
  • Subscription information: Managed by RevenueCat (revenuecat.com). We receive only your subscription status (active/inactive). We do not receive your payment details.
  • App preferences: Settings such as blocked prefixes and dark mode preference, stored locally on your device.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifier.
  • We do not read the content of your calls or messages.
  • We do not upload your blocklist or call history to any server.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Permissions We Request

  • Call Screening / Answer Calls: Required to identify and block incoming calls. We do not record call content.
  • Notifications: Used to notify you when a call is blocked. Optional.

4. Third-Party Services

RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous usage data to process your purchase and validate your subscription. Please review RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Google Play Billing: Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data (blocklist, call history, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

6. Children's Privacy

CallBlockery is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
